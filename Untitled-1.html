My Favorite Singer IU

Introduction and her singing career

My favorite singer name is IU. Her real name is Lee Ji-Eun. She is South Korean singer, songwriter and actress. She was born on May 16, 1993. She is very reknowed in South Korea. She was very passionate about singing from her childhood. Due to her family's poor financial conditon, she and her brother have to live under the care of their grandmother away from their parents. During her middle school days, she used to sing in the functions of the school and got applauded and decided to become singer in the future. She gave around 20 auditinas to become the trainee of entrainment company but failled to do so. She was once scamed by fake entertainment comapany to become trainee. Later, she joined LOEN entertainment, where she got training to become singer.

In 2007, she signed with LOEN Entertainment (now Kakao Entertainment) as a trainee. And after recieving training for nine months finally in 2008, she made her debut with her first extended play (EP) "Lost and Found". She then released her two other albums "Growing up" and "IU...IM" which was sucessfull in streaming platform. But it was her "Good day", a lead single from her album "Real" brought her massive sucess and made her nation favorite singer. "Good day" spent at the top for five consucative week in South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart and in 2019, it was ranked on the Billboard's 100 greatest K-pop song of the 2010's list.

In 2011, IU released her two albums,"Real+" and "Last Fantasy" which was major hit and charted high in the music charts of South Korea. Due to her sucess, she created her image as "girl next door" and got title "Korea's Little Sister". In the same year, she released her self-written song "Hold My Hand" which was OST for the television series "The greatest Love". This song was also a huge hit and charted high in the music chart.

With her third studio album "Modern Times" released in 2013, showed her more mature music style compared to her previous albums which were girlish type. The several tracks of this album chareted in the top 10 of Gaon Digital Chart. For the first time, she wrote lyrics for all the song in the album and got creadited as sole lyricist for this album.

IU's fouth studio album, "Palette" which was released on 2017, became her first album to reach number one in the Billboard's World Album Chart. She continueosly retained her dominace on the music chart of South Korea with her following record "Love Poeam" and "Lialic" In 2020, she released her song "Eight" which became her first song to reach number one on the Billboard World Digital Song sale's Chart.

Her Acting Career

Aside from her sucessful music career, she has also sucessful career in acting industry. IU first debuted as supporting role in teen drama "Dream High" in 2011 and appeared on several television show as minor charecter. She then appeared as leading role in television series like "You are the Best" (2013), "Pretty Man" (2013-14), "The Producers" (2015) and "Moon lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo" (2016).

In 2018, her role as a desperate office worker in "My Mister" recieved critical acclaim and earned her first Best Actress in Televison nomination at 55th Beaksang Arts Awards. In 2018, she starred in anthology film "Series Persona" and fantasy televison series "Hotel Del Luna", which led her second time nomination for Best Actress at the Beaksang Awards. In 2022, IU starred in film Broker directed by Hirokazu Kore-Eda alongside Song Kang-Ho, Bae Doona and Gang Dong-Won.

Her Achievements

  1. IU is the one of the best selling solo artist in K-pop industry. She performed at the Olympic Gymnastic during the Seoul leg of her 2019 "Love, Poeam" concert tour which made her first solo female K-pop act to perform there.
  2. She is first Korean female artist to hold a solo concert, "The Golden hour" at Seoul Olympic Stadium in Seoul which was held on September 17 and 18, 2022.
  3. She was named by Rolling Stone the 135th greatest singer of all time in 2023 ranking
  4. Since 2012, Forbes magazine included her in the top ten annual Korea Powerful celebraty list and attained peak ranking of number three this year.
  5. In 2014, Billboard recognized IU as the all time leader of its K-pop Hot 100 with the most number of the songs and artist with the most weeks at the number-one position on the chart.
  6. In 2014 and 2017, she was also named as Gallup Korea's Singer.
